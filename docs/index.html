Capstone Design 2025-2

3D Reconstruction
Performance Analysis

Investigating the impact of compression artifacts on 3D Gaussian Splatting and VGGT reconstruction quality. A comprehensive study utilizing COLMAP SfM automation.

View Analysis Results

Project Abstract

Understanding the intersection of compression and 3D geometry.

The Challenge

Standard image compression algorithms introduce artifacts that may degrade the quality of 3D reconstructions. We aim to quantify this degradation across modern pipelines.

The Approach

We automated the COLMAP SfM pipeline and tested reconstructions using both 3D Gaussian Splatting and VGGT on datasets with varying levels of compression.

Key Technologies

3D Gaussian Splatting

Real-time radiance field rendering using 3D Gaussians. State-of-the-art view synthesis quality.

VGGT

Visual Geometry Grounded Transformer. A novel approach for 3D understanding and reconstruction.
